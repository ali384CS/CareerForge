# Next-Gen Job Hunting App Principles

When building or modifying features for this application, always adhere to the following product and design principles:

1. **Move Beyond Simple ATS**: Do not rely on simple keyword matching. Provide actionable, role-specific insights (e.g., impact rewrites with metrics).
2. **Be the Brutally Honest Coach**: Analyze gaps, job hopping, or missing skills critically, and provide automated solutions or explanations (e.g., 1-line gap explainers, free courses for missing skills).
3. **Application Agent Automation**: Focus on deep personalization over generic applications (e.g., auto-tailored cover letters, tracking versions, predicting interview questions).
4. **Proactive Job Discovery**: Aim to find hidden opportunities, recent postings (< 24h), and referral paths rather than just generic job board scraping.
5. **Dynamic "Conversation First" UX**: The UI should feel alive and conversational. Prioritize chat-based interactions over traditional forms where possible. 
6. **Maintain Memory**: Ensure the system remembers previous jobs applied to, user responses, and preferences across sessions.
7. **Premium Aesthetics**: Ensure the design feels cutting-edge (dark modes, glassmorphism, micro-animations) and highly polished. Do not settle for basic MVP UI.
